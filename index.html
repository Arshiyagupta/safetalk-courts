Step 1: Submit Your Concern

Co-Parent 1 sends a text message to submit a new concern. Co-Parent 1 can communicate privately with the digital parenting coordinator without Co-Parent 2 seeing the conversation.

Step 2: Provide Details

Co-Parent 1 types their concern and can be as thorough and honest as needed. The digital parenting coordinator needs complete information to help effectively.

Step 3: Coordinator Gathers Information

Your digital parenting coordinator responds with empathetic clarifying questions in a judgment-free environment. Co-Parent 1 can answer as thoroughly and honestly as possible in this emotionally safer space.

Step 4: Continue Discussion

Your digital parenting coordinator continues gathering information from Co-Parent 1 through empathetic follow-up questions to fully understand the situation. Your digital parenting coordinator acts as a supportive guide for Co-Parent 1 - no emotional escalation, just professional assistance.

Step 5: Coordinator Reviews Situation

Your digital parenting coordinator continues the conversation with Co-Parent 1 to gather all necessary details about the concern. Co-Parent 1 can say what's on their mind and process their feelings - your digital parenting coordinator helps Co-Parent 1 express things in a way that feels safe and respectful.

Step 6: Coordinator Contacts Co-Parent 2

When your digital parenting coordinator has gathered all necessary information from Co-Parent 1, they will contact Co-Parent 2 with complete neutrality. The coordinator presents the concern professionally and objectively.

Step 7: Coordinator Shares Your Concern

Your digital parenting coordinator shared Co-Parent 1's concern with Co-Parent 2. Co-Parent 1 never has to message their ex directly - your digital parenting coordinator acts as a professional go-between. Co-Parent 1 waits for Co-Parent 2's insights with no risk of immediate trigger for escalation.

Step 8: Proposed Solution Presented

Your digital parenting coordinator presents a proposed solution to Co-Parent 2 based on both parents' input. Co-Parent 2 reviews the solution and provides feedback on whether it works for their situation.

Step 9: Solution Shared with Co-Parent 1

Co-Parent 2 agreed and the solution is now shared with Co-Parent 1. Your digital parenting coordinator ensures both parents understand the agreed-upon approach and are aligned on next steps.

Step 10: Both Parents Agree

Both parents have agreed and need to follow through with what they agreed on. Your digital parenting coordinator has successfully facilitated a resolution that works for both parties and prioritizes your child's well-being.
